# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to making participation in pylopdf a respectful,
harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of background, identity,
experience level, or personal characteristics.

## Expected behavior

Examples of behavior that contributes to a healthy project include:

- being respectful, patient, and constructive;
- focusing criticism on code, behavior, evidence, and tradeoffs;
- acknowledging mistakes and correcting them promptly;
- respecting privacy, licenses, and the sensitivity of documents shared in bug
  reports;
- helping create an environment where people can ask questions safely.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, threats, discrimination, sexualized
attention, personal attacks, deliberate intimidation, publishing another
person's private information, or sustained disruption of project work.

## Scope

This policy applies in project spaces and when someone is publicly representing
the project, including issues, pull requests, discussions, release channels,
and other project-related communication.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to the maintainer at `yusuke8h@gmail.com`.
Reports will be reviewed promptly and handled as confidentially as practical.
Project maintainers may edit or remove contributions and may temporarily or
permanently restrict participation when behavior violates this policy.

Enforcement decisions will consider context, severity, repetition, impact, and
whether the participant takes responsibility and corrects the behavior.
